Products - Rubber Floor Tiles

Rubber Floor Tiles also called Rubber Floor Coverings are made of synthetic rubber which has been moulded under pressure and vulcanized at high temperature.

General Characteristics

Wear-resistance of Rubber Floor Tiles is long useful lifetime meaning less frequent need of disposal. It is resistant to chemical which the flooring suffer no demage from short-time exposure to solvents, diluted acids and alkalis. Moreover, Rubber Floor Tiles are resistant to cigarette burns that prevent surface damage. Rubber Floor Tiles are made for foot fall sound absorption and result to less noise even heavy traffic. It is permanently resilient with lasting high walking comfort. Rubber Floor Tiles are dimensionally stable which ensure that the tiles do not contain any plasticizers, therefore no shrinkage. No coating required for the floor tiles meaning substantially reduced cared and maintenance costs and less pollutants in the waste water.

Exemplary Environmental Friendly

For the raw materials to the production process, from installation to disposal, our rubber floor tiles are environmental-friendly, and thus offer more than just their superlative quality. Beside, our Rubber Floor Tiles do not contain Halogens, Plasticizers, Formaldehyde, Asbestos, Cadmium, CFCs.
